A timer to track focusing in 25 minute intervals, which is a good length of time to stay focused before taking a small break.

  1. Write your task
  2. Make a promise to yourself
  3. Click the start button

The idea is based on the Pomodoro Technique (wiki), except that short and long breaks are not tracked.
